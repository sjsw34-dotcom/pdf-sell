### What Your Numbers Mean

- **3+ of one element = Dominant.** That element's energy is loud in your life. You naturally express those traits — maybe even too much. Think of it as your default setting turned up to maximum.

- **0 of an element = Missing.** This doesn't mean you're broken. It means that element's energy doesn't come naturally to you. You may need to actively seek it — through people, environments, habits, or career choices.

- **1–2 of each element = Balanced.** This is rare and genuinely harmonious. You have access to all five energies without any one dominating. The trade-off? You may feel like a generalist rather than a specialist.
