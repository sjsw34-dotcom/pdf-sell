# How Saju Works

Saju (사주, "Four Pillars") is Korea's time-tested system for understanding who you are and where your life is headed. It takes the exact moment you were born and converts it into a chart of cosmic symbols. That chart is your blueprint.

No zodiac wheels. No rising signs. Just four pillars built from your birth year, month, day, and hour.

## The Four Pillars at a Glance

Each pillar has two rows:

- **Heavenly Stem** (천간) — the top row, representing outward energy
- **Earthly Branch** (지지) — the bottom row, representing deeper, hidden energy

That gives you **eight characters** total. Here is what a sample chart looks like:

|  | Hour Pillar | Day Pillar | Month Pillar | Year Pillar |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Heavenly Stem** | Jeong (丁) Fire | Gap (甲) Wood | Byeong (丙) Fire | Im (壬) Water |
| **Earthly Branch** | Myo (卯) Rabbit | O (午) Horse | In (寅) Tiger | Sin (申) Monkey |

Read it right to left: Year → Month → Day → Hour. Each pillar captures a different layer of your life.

> *This is a structural sample for learning purposes — it may not correspond to an actual calendar date. Use the lookup tables in Chapters 2–3 to build your real chart.*

## What Is a Day Master?

The most important single character in your chart is the **Heavenly Stem of the Day Pillar**. That is your **Day Master** (일간).

In the sample chart above, the Day Master is **Gap (甲) — Yang Wood**. Think of it as your core element, the lens through which you experience everything.

There are 10 possible Day Masters, each with a distinct personality. You will meet all 10 in Chapter 4.
