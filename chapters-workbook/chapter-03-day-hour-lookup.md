## Part 1: The Day Pillar

Here is the honest truth: the Day Pillar is the hardest one to find by hand.

The Day Pillar follows the same 60-pair sexagenary cycle as the Year Pillar, but it changes **every single day**. There is no simple formula — traditionally, you need a **Ten Thousand Year Calendar** (만세력, manseryeok), a massive reference book that lists the Stem-Branch pair for every day going back centuries.

Calculating it manually requires counting the total number of days from a known reference date and finding its position in the 60-day cycle. It is doable but tedious and error-prone.

**The good news:** sajumuse.com calculates your exact Day Pillar instantly from your birth date. Just enter your birthday and you will have it in seconds.

## Part 2: The Hour Pillar

The Hour Pillar is more straightforward. Like the Month Pillar, it has a fixed Branch (based on time) and a variable Stem (based on your Day Stem).

### Hour Branch — Fixed by Birth Time

Saju divides the day into twelve 2-hour blocks, each assigned to one Earthly Branch. The day starts at 23:00 (11 PM), not midnight.

| Time Range | Branch | Animal |
|---|---|---|
| 23:00 – 00:59 | Ja (子) | Rat |
| 01:00 – 02:59 | Chuk (丑) | Ox |
| 03:00 – 04:59 | In (寅) | Tiger |
| 05:00 – 06:59 | Myo (卯) | Rabbit |
| 07:00 – 08:59 | Jin (辰) | Dragon |
| 09:00 – 10:59 | Sa (巳) | Snake |
| 11:00 – 12:59 | O (午) | Horse |
| 13:00 – 14:59 | Mi (未) | Goat |
| 15:00 – 16:59 | Sin (申) | Monkey |
| 17:00 – 18:59 | Yu (酉) | Rooster |
| 19:00 – 20:59 | Sul (戌) | Dog |
| 21:00 – 22:59 | Hae (亥) | Pig |

> **Heads up:** If you were born between 23:00 and 00:59, your Hour Branch is Ja (Rat), but in Saju this time block belongs to the **next day's** Day Pillar. This matters for accuracy.

### Hour Stem — Determined by Day Stem

Just like the Month Stem depends on the Year Stem, the Hour Stem depends on your **Day Stem**. The Ja (Rat) hour gets a starting stem, and then the stems cycle forward through the twelve hours.

**Starting stem rule:**

| Day Stem Group | Ja (子) Hour starts with |
|---|---|
| Gap (甲) or Gi (己) | Gap (甲) |
| Eul (乙) or Gyeong (庚) | Byeong (丙) |
| Byeong (丙) or Sin (辛) | Mu (戊) |
| Jeong (丁) or Im (壬) | Gyeong (庚) |
| Mu (戊) or Gye (癸) | Im (壬) |

| **Jeong/Im day** | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin |
| **Mu/Gye day** | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye |

### Putting It Together

**Example:** Your Day Stem is Gap (甲) and you were born at 3:30 PM.

1. 3:30 PM = 15:30 → **Sin (申) Monkey** hour
2. Gap belongs to the **Gap/Gi** group → Sin hour stem is **Im (壬)**
3. Hour Pillar = **Im (壬) Sin (申)** — Yang Water Monkey
