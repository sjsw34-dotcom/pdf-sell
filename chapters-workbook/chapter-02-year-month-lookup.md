# Year & Month Pillar Lookup Tables

Your first two pillars — Year and Month — are the easiest to find. All you need is a table and your birth date.

## Part 1: The Year Pillar

The Year Pillar follows a 60-year cycle called the **sexagenary cycle** (육십갑자). Each year gets a unique Heavenly Stem + Earthly Branch pair. The cycle repeats every 60 years.

**Important:** In Saju, the year starts at the **lunar new year** (usually early February), not January 1st. If you were born in January or early February, your Saju year may be the previous calendar year. Check the exact lunar new year date for your birth year.

### How to Read the Table

Find your birth year. The columns give you:
- **Stem** — your Year Heavenly Stem (천간)
- **Branch** — your Year Earthly Branch with its animal (지지)
- **Element** — the element of your Year Stem

## Part 2: The Month Pillar

The Month Pillar has two parts: the **Month Branch** (fixed by the month) and the **Month Stem** (determined by your Year Stem).

### Month Branch — Fixed by Lunar Month

Each lunar month is permanently assigned to one Earthly Branch. The solar date ranges below are approximate — lunar boundaries shift slightly each year.

| Lunar Month | Branch (Animal) | Approx. Solar Dates |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1 (정월) | In (寅) Tiger | Feb 4 – Mar 5 |
| Month 2 | Myo (卯) Rabbit | Mar 6 – Apr 4 |
| Month 3 | Jin (辰) Dragon | Apr 5 – May 5 |
| Month 4 | Sa (巳) Snake | May 6 – Jun 5 |
| Month 5 | O (午) Horse | Jun 6 – Jul 6 |
| Month 6 | Mi (未) Goat | Jul 7 – Aug 7 |
| Month 7 | Sin (申) Monkey | Aug 8 – Sep 7 |
| Month 8 | Yu (酉) Rooster | Sep 8 – Oct 7 |
| Month 9 | Sul (戌) Dog | Oct 8 – Nov 6 |
| Month 10 | Hae (亥) Pig | Nov 7 – Dec 6 |
| Month 11 | Ja (子) Rat | Dec 7 – Jan 5 |
| Month 12 | Chuk (丑) Ox | Jan 6 – Feb 3 |

> **Note:** These dates follow the solar terms (절기), which are used in Saju for month boundaries. They are more precise than the lunar calendar dates.

### Month Stem — Determined by Year Stem

The Heavenly Stem of each month follows a pattern based on your **Year Stem**. The first month (In/Tiger, ~February) gets a starting stem, and then the stems cycle forward in order.

**Starting stem rule:**

| Year Stem Group | Month 1 (In) starts with |
|---|---|
| Gap (甲) or Gi (己) | Byeong (丙) |
| Eul (乙) or Gyeong (庚) | Mu (戊) |
| Byeong (丙) or Sin (辛) | Gyeong (庚) |
| Jeong (丁) or Im (壬) | Im (壬) |
| Mu (戊) or Gye (癸) | Gap (甲) |

### Full Month Stem Table

From the starting stem, each subsequent month advances one stem. Here is the complete table:

| Month → | M1 (In) | M2 (Myo) | M3 (Jin) | M4 (Sa) | M5 (O) | M6 (Mi) | M7 (Sin) | M8 (Yu) | M9 (Sul) | M10 (Hae) | M11 (Ja) | M12 (Chuk) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Gap/Gi year** | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong |
| **Eul/Gyeong year** | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi |
| **Byeong/Sin year** | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin |
| **Jeong/Im year** | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye |
| **Mu/Gye year** | Gap | Eul | Byeong | Jeong | Mu | Gi | Gyeong | Sin | Im | Gye | Gap | Eul |

### Putting It Together

**Example:** Born March 20, 1995.

1. Year Stem is **Eul (乙)** (from the Year Pillar table)
2. March 20 falls in Month 2 (Myo/Rabbit, Mar 6 – Apr 4)
3. Eul belongs to the **Eul/Gyeong** group → Month 2 stem is **Gi (己)**
4. Month Pillar = **Gi (己) Myo (卯)** — Yin Earth Rabbit
