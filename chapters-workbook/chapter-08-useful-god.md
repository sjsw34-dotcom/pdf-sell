# Find Your Useful God (用神)

If your Saju chart were a recipe, the Useful God (Yongsin, 용신) would be the missing ingredient. It's the element your chart **needs most** — the one that brings everything into balance.

Think of it this way: if your chart is heavy on Wood and Water but has zero Fire, you might feel directionless or invisible no matter how hard you work. Adding Fire energy — through career choices, colors, habits, even the direction you face — can shift everything.

The Useful God is one of the most important concepts in professional Saju reading. What follows is a simplified way to estimate yours.

## Three Steps to Find Your Useful God

### Step 1: Know Your Day Master Element

### Step 2: Know Your Birth Season

The season you were born in determines whether your Day Master is naturally **strong** or **weak** — because elements gain or lose power depending on the season.

| Season | Months | Strongest Element |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Spring | February – April | Wood (thriving season) |
| Summer | May – July | Fire (thriving season) |
| Autumn | August – October | Metal (thriving season) |
| Winter | November – January | Water (thriving season) |

**My Birth Season: _______________**

**General rule:** If your Day Master element matches or is fed by the season's element, your Day Master is likely **strong**. If the season weakens or controls your element, your Day Master is likely **weak**.

Once you know your Useful God, you can actively invite that energy into your life. Here's a practical guide:

| Earth | Yellow, brown, beige, ochre | Center | Sweet flavors, root vegetables, grains | Real estate, agriculture, consulting, HR |

### Practical Ways to Use This

You don't have to overhaul your life. Small, consistent choices add up:

- **Wardrobe:** Add your Useful God's colors to your daily rotation. Even accessories count — a watch, phone case, or bag.
- **Workspace:** Face your Useful God's direction when you work. Decorate with that element's colors.
- **Diet:** Lean into the associated flavors. Not as a strict diet — just a gentle preference.
- **Career:** If you're choosing between paths, lean toward the sector that matches your Useful God. You'll find more natural flow there.
- **Environment:** Spend time in spaces that embody the element. Water = near rivers or the ocean. Wood = forests and parks. Fire = sunny, bright spaces. Earth = gardens and grounded spaces. Metal = clean, minimalist, structured environments.

## An Honest Caveat

This is a simplified assessment based on two factors: your Day Master element and your birth season. The real deal is significantly more nuanced.

A precise Useful God analysis requires examining:

- All eight characters in your chart, not just the Day Master
- Hidden stems within each Earthly Branch
- The relative strength of every element (not just strong/weak, but by degree)
- Special formations, clashes, and combinations that alter element dynamics
- Your current fortune cycle, which can temporarily shift what you need

Two people with the same Day Master born in the same season can have completely different Useful Gods based on the rest of their chart. This table gives you a starting point — not a diagnosis.
