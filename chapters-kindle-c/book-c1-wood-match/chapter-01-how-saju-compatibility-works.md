# How Saju Compatibility Works

You've probably been told you're "compatible" or "incompatible" with someone based on your zodiac signs. Aries and Libra. Scorpio and Taurus. Good match. Bad match. Simple labels that give simple answers to a complicated question.

Saju doesn't work that way.

In Korean Four Pillars astrology, compatibility isn't a label — it's a *dynamic*. A specific, describable, predictable set of interactions between two people's elemental systems. Every pairing has chemistry. Every pairing has friction. What matters isn't the label. What matters is whether you understand the dynamic well enough to work with it.

This book gives you that understanding — specifically for your Wood element.

## The Three Layers of Compatibility

When two people's charts interact, three things happen simultaneously:

### Layer 1: Elemental Relationship

The most fundamental layer. Your Day Master element and their Day Master element create one of three basic relationships:

**Productive relationship:** One element feeds the other. Wood feeds Fire. Water feeds Wood. These pairings have a natural flow of energy — one person nourishes the other. The question is whether the flow is reciprocal or one-directional.

**Controlling relationship:** One element restrains the other. Metal cuts Wood. Wood controls Earth. These pairings have built-in tension — one person's strength is the other's challenge. The question is whether the tension produces growth or resentment.

**Parallel relationship:** Same element meets same element. Wood meets Wood. These pairings have instant recognition but also competition. The question is whether two trees grow together or fight for the same sunlight.

### Layer 2: Yin-Yang Polarity

Every Day Master is either Yang (active, outward, initiating) or Yin (receptive, inward, responding). The polarity combination affects the dynamic significantly:

- **Yang + Yin** of the same element: complementary. Different expressions of the same energy.
- **Yang + Yang:** both initiating. Can create power struggles.
- **Yin + Yin:** both receptive. Can create passivity — neither person drives the relationship forward.
- **Yang + Yin** of different elements: the most naturally dynamic combination. Different energies, complementary polarities.

### Layer 3: Full Chart Interaction

Your Day Master is just one of eight characters in your chart. The other elements in your chart interact with the other elements in their chart, creating a web of relationships that's unique to the specific pair. Two Wood Day Masters with completely different supporting elements will experience very different compatibility patterns.

This book focuses on Layer 1 — the Day Master to Day Master dynamic — because it's the most accessible and the most immediately useful. For the full Layer 3 picture, a professional compatibility reading maps all eight characters against each other.

## The Wood Compatibility Baseline

Before we dive into specific pairings, here's what you bring to every relationship as a Wood person:

**Your relational superpower:** Vision. You see where things are going — the relationship, the business, the friendship. You provide direction, momentum, and the confidence that things are moving forward.

**Your relational challenge:** Control. Your directional clarity can become directional imposition. "I know where we should go" slides easily into "we're going where I say." Learning to share the steering wheel is Wood's lifelong relationship curriculum.

**What you need from others:** Growth. You need relationships that are going somewhere. Stagnant relationships — even comfortable ones — slowly kill your connection to the other person.

**What you give to others:** Structure and ambition. People around you feel pulled forward, challenged to be more, inspired to build. Your energy is contagious — and sometimes exhausting.
